A DISADVANTAGE OF THE GENERAL SUBSTITUTION CIPHER IS THAT BOTH SENDER
AND RECEIVER MUST COMMIT THE PERMUTED CIPHER SEQUENCE TO MEMORY. A COMMON
TECHNIQUE FOR AVOIDING THIS IS TO USE A KEYWORD FROM WHICH THE CIPHER SEQUENCE
CAN BE GENERATED. FOR EXAMPLE, USING THE KEYWORD CIPHER, WRITE OUT
THE KEYWORD FOLLOWED BY UNUSED LETTERS IN NORMAL ORDER AND MATCH THIS AGAINST
THE PLAINTEXT LETTERS
